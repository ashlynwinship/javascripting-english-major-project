New York Cities

A comparison of interpretations of the city

This website features locations referenced in the book Open City by Teju Cole, as well as maps of my own journeys through New York City. This site is my final project for my Digital Literary Studies: Novel Maps of NY class.

Map Key: My derive locations are marked with teardrops. My first derive is in orange and my second is in purple; the red lines indicate places I went right before or after the derives that I still found interesting enough to point out. The yellow area represents where I spend most of my time. The Open City character Julius's locations are marked with circles.
