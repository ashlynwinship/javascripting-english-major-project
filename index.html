What does it mean to live in a city?

It's highly unlikely that anyone could ever completely know New York City. But if you spend your time in the city completely oblivious to everything except your necessities, can you claim to know it at all? If you take the N train to work every day but can't speak with any knowledge about the stops beyond your own, or if you walk home on University to 12th Street and have no idea what might be on 13th, then you're not really living in New York City. You're living in your own city.
